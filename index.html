About

My name is Andrew Miles and I have learned how to design websites using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for my Work-Based Learning class. My plan was to first learn how to complete this portfolio and then make a website for my school's Interact Club and an AI generated website. I ended up creating both this portfolio and the Interact Club website while chosing to learn more about data collection, manipulation, and storage rather than explore AI. For this portfolio, I may have consulted AI but I only used it to help me generate my own unique ideas and solutions. I previously knew Java, Python, and C# which helped me understand the logic behind HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I hope to possibly use these newfound skills of website creation and design to stay competitive in the job market and find solutions when working at my future job. My main goal was to learn how to make websites in HTML, CSS, and Javascript and I believe I have met and surpassed this goal as I now know these languages and how to use them for different implementations including collecting data.
